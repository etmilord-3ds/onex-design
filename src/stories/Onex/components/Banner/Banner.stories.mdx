## Banner

A banner is a prominent message with related actions.

### Banner variants

The component has 3 variants such as "info", "warning", "error".

##### <i>information</i>

Use <b>information</b> banner to show general product announcements, e.g., significant updates or releases.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Info" story={stories.Info}/>
</Canvas>

##### <i>warning</i>

Use <b>warning</b> banners to provide info that some issue might happen in the nearest future or when a specific action is required from the user.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Warning" story={stories.Warning}/>
</Canvas>

##### <i>error</i>

Use <b>error</b> banner to inform the user about critical issues.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Error" story={stories.Error}/>
</Canvas>
